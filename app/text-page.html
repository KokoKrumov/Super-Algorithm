Customer privacy statement

Your privacy is very important to us. We have drafted this Privacy Statement to inform you about how we use data about you when you use our services. We are Crowd Analyzer Inc. Our registered office address is at Boutique Villa #7, Dubai Knowledge Village, Al Sufouh Road, Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

  1. General

    1. This Privacy Statement explains how we collect, process and use information about you. For the purposes of this Privacy Statement, you means any user who uses any of the services that we offer, including visitors to our website, users of Crowd Analyzer Platform, our social media monitoring application, and any other services we may offer from time to time (collectively, "Services"). This Privacy Statement applies to all users, including those users who use the Services via their employer or another company.
    2. This Privacy Statement applies to all of the information that we collect through our Services, irrespective of the type of Services or the device on which you access the Services.
    3. We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time to clarify or amend our privacy practices. If we make a material change to this Privacy Statement, we will notify you either by email, notice on or through the Services, or in some other way. Your continued use of the Services constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Statement.

  2. Collection

    1. The personal data we collect about you depends on the Services you use. For example, if you are the primary account holder for your company and use Crowd Analyzer Platform, the data we collect about you would be different than that which we collect about a user who only accesses Crowd Analyzer App through Hootsuite.
